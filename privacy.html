Privacy Policy

Introduction

ChickenMath ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our ChickenMath mobile application.

Information We Collect

ChickenMath collects the following types of information:

  • Chicken Data: Information about your chickens including names, breeds, hatch dates, and health notes
  • Egg Production Data: Daily egg counts and production records
  • Reminder Settings: Your custom reminder preferences for chicken care tasks
  • App Settings: Your preferences for app functionality and appearance

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the ChickenMath app functionality
  • Generate analytics and insights about your flock's performance
  • Send you reminders and notifications you've configured
  • Improve our app features and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally backed up to iCloud if you enable iCloud backup. We do not have access to your personal data stored in iCloud. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

ChickenMath may use the following third-party services:

  • iCloud: For data backup and synchronization (if enabled)
  • Apple Analytics: For app usage analytics and crash reporting

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable iCloud backup at any time
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Children's Privacy

ChickenMath does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
